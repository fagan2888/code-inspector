Miss Mint url

2009

I built Miss Mint the weekend after building Flossed Today. I leveraged the Flossed Today framework to jump start development on Miss Mint. It grew quickly solely by word of mouth - growing to 1 million reminders sent.

MissMint.com employed technologies such as:

  • Sinatra
  • Datamapper
  • SMS delivery via a redundant system of Amazon SES and GMail.
  • Receiving/Parsing of SMS messages with mms2r
  • Google Charts API

Miss Mint launched April 2009. Two years later, I released a native iPhone and Android app. These were built with Titanium Mobile.

I sold Miss Mint in March of 2012 to meric.
